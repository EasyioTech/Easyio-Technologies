# The Science of Movement

Global logistics is a game of coordination. Our **Supply Chain Ledger** brings transparency to the most complex movements.

## Immutable Tracking

We implemented an **Event-Sourced Ledger** that ensures every touchpoint is recorded and unchangeable.

- **GPS Integration**: Real-time position tracking across multi-modal transport (Road, Air, Sea).
- **Warehouse Optimization**: Intelligent bin-tracking to reduce delay in fulfillment.
- **Auto-Sync Status**: The ledger updates all stakeholders instantly as milestones are reached.

- **Total Transparency**: An immutable record of the entire supply chain journey.
